Architecture

How It Fits Together

Each tool connects to the same source of truth - the Excel OutputTable named range - but consumes it differently for its own audience.

01
Excel
All assumptions in Inputs tab. Named range OutputTable exports 15 metrics × 5 years - the single source of truth.
02
Power BI
Connects via Power Query. What-If Parameters and DAX measures run scenario analysis. Published for the internal deal team.
03
Tableau
Connects to the same OutputTable independently. Board-presentation polish. Published to Tableau Public.
04
HTML / JS
Reimplements every formula in vanilla JavaScript. No external dependency. Works offline. The guaranteed fallback.
